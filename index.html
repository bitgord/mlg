About Us

Expert blockchain development and consulting


MLG Blockchain is a blockchain development and consulting firm based in Toronto that is focused on building next generation applications using blockchain and smart contract technology. We fast track you're team's understanding of the blockchain and its potential opportunities for your business and help you to create a blockchain strategy you can use today.

We are blockchain agnostic and have experience working with many blockchain fabrics including the Bitcoin Blockchain, Ethereum, Hyperledger, Ripple, Factom, Eris. We also have experience working with many blockchain development APIs, decentralized storage protocols and different blockchain tools for for testing, building and devOps.
